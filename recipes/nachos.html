Nacho Recipe

Description

This is a basic nacho recipe passed down from my great great abuelita. It reminds me when I was a kid coming home to the smell of refried beans from a can and corn tortilla chips fresh from the Kroger branded bag. Mixed with some sour cream and some cheese, and you got yourself one heck of a nachos night.

Ingredients

  • Refried Beans
  • Kroger branded chips
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream

Here's how to make it:

  1. Open a can of refried beans. It should have the consistency of wet dog food.
  2. Heat them up in a microwaveable bowl that will be too hot to touch while keeping the beans cold
  3. Open a bag of Kroger branded chips and pour them into an oversized plate
  4. Remove beans from bowl and slosh onto chips
  5. Sprinkle cheese on top and place in microwave long enough to make the cheese burn onto the chips
  6. Remove and add desired amount of sour cream (for flavor)
  7. Enjoy!
