Chili Recipe

Description

Now I'm no fancy cook, but this chili recipe may be one of the greatest creations on gods green earth. No lie! It'll take you back to those winter days walking to school in 15 feet of snow and not complaining about it days. Y'know, the good ole days!

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Different Beans
  • Brown Sugar
  • A pinch of love
  • Hella sour cream and shredded cheese

Steps to make:

  1. Pour your beans and different beans into a giant pot. The bigger the pot, the better the flavor.
  2. Pour half a bag of brown sugar, and then some more into the pot as it simmers.
  3. Take your pinch of love and add it in 5 minutes before being done.
  4. Pour into a bowl
  5. Put as much cheese and sour cream as humanly possible.
  6. Enjoy!
