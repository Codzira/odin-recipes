Pizza Recipe

Description

This is how to make my great great great italian grandmothers ooey gooey pizza recipe. All you need are store bought ingredients from your local farmers gas station and a dream and you'll be dining on some classic italian cuisine in no time!

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Doritos nachos dip
  • Pepperonis (Pepperoni flavored combos is fine if needed)
  • A dream

Steps to make:

  1. Open bread and line on a baking sheet pre covered in pam cooking oil
  2. Open can of doritos nachos dip spread and lather it up baby
  3. Taking Pepperoni or Pepperoni suppliment and sprinkle generously
  4. Insert dream
  5. Bake in 500 degree oven for 2 hours
  6. Enjoy!
